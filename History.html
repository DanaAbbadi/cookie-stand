History

Pat's Salmon Cookies is a Seattle Staple! It has grown to be loved by many locals and tourists for it's cozy atmosphere and quirky cookies. We release a new cookie recipe (with salmon of course!) twice a year during our anniversary celebration! Look out for our new flavor this year due to come out on July 5th, and if enough people vote for it, it will stay in our menu year-round!
